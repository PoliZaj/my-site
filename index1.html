Experience the power of hip-hop and grafitti

Romeo and Juliet is the performance combining dance, graffiti, and hip hop music. We believe that this form of the event will encourage the young audience to discover hip hop culture and the everlasting story of love, both expressed by the power of hip-hop culture. Loud, rebellion and energetic are the best words to describe our event. We are working with the most innovative and inspirational artists specializing in hip-hop dance, including mindblowing and impressive breakdance style.

We collaborate with famous graffiti artists who have created a fantastic scenography, especially for this event, reflecting the atmosphere of hip-hop culture. You will hear some of the most important pieces of music performed by world-famous artists. If you love hip-hop as we do, many of them you already know. If hip hop is strange to you, let us surprise you. Feel free to join us, be a part of the rebellion! And don't forget to leave your spray paint can at home... just for this time...

Snoop dog is one of the most famous American rappers. In collaboration with Dr. Dre, the first album was founded in 1992 and turned out to be a huge success and the beginning of his solo career. Award-winning, his albums rank very high in the charts.

Dr. Dre is an American rapper and producer. He promoted many famous artists of the same trend. When creating music, he uses a piano and drums, which gives his work a great style. One of the most important representatives of the hip-hop.

50 Cent is an American rapper, producer, and entrepreneur. Gained international acclaim with his first album, "Get Rich or Die Tryin," in 2003. He releases albums in collaboration with other rappers. His heavy musical style is very recognizable.

Eminem is an American rapper, producer, and actor. Member of the hip-hop group D12 and a fifteen-time Grammy winner. The "Roling Stones" magazine ranked him on 83 on the list of 100 Greatest Artists of All Time. 240 million CDs sold worldwide.
